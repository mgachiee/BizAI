# BizAI: The Future of Loaning Support for MSMEs

Micro, Small, and Medium Enterprises (MSMEs) frequently face difficulties accessing loans due to limited financial history and perceived risk by lenders. This paper proposes a GenAI-powered chatbot to address these challenges by providing tailored insights into loan options, financial planning, and cash flow management.
